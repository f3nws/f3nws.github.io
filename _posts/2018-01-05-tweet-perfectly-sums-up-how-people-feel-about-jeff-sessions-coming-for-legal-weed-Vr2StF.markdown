U.S. Attorney General Jeff Sessions had a New Year's surprise for America: he's coming for your legal weed. Defying the will of the voters, Sessions announced on Thursday a rollback of policies put in place by the Obama administration that protects states that legalize cannabis. The announcement comes just days after California officially launched recreational cannabis sales, and stands to become the largest market for legal weed in the world.
