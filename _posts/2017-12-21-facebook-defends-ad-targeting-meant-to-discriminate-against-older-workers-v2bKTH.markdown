In an ad featuring a smiling millennial woman, Verizon promises “your [sic] more than just a number,” a corporate euphemism meant to relay a company values its employees. In this case, it’s an ironic choice of words. Whether the sentiment was true or not is irrelevant; the ad only appeared in the feeds of those aged 25 to 36.
