Pakistan unveiled the remains of a 1,700-year-old 14-metre-(48-foot)-high Kanjur stone Buddha image on Wednesday. The 14-metre-(48-foot)-high Kanjur stone Buddha image was unearthed from the ancient Buddhist site in Bhamala province.
