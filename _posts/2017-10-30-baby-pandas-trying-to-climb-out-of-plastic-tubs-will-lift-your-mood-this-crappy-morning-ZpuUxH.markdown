So this is how baby pandas are kept apart before bedtime. These babies are at the China Conservation and Research Center for the Giant Panda, in Sichuan. The center posted this now-viral video to social media, showing them waiting in plastic tubs for their keepers. A few of them clumsily try to escape, and if you pay attention, you'll see a little one in the back succeed and tumble out of his tub at the end. Oh, and keep the sound on.
