As the value of Bitcoin soars, it also turns holders of large volumes of the currency into prime targets for thieves. That’s what appears to have done in NiceHash, a Slovenia-based Bitcoin exchange that claims its cryptocurrency wallet has been cleared out of $64 million worth of BTC. For those keeping score, that’s about 4,700 BTC, each worth about $13,617 at the time of the breach. That isn’t from NiceHash itself, but rather from a wallet address circulated by NiceHash users.
