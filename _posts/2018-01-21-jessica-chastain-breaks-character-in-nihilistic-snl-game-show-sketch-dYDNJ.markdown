In a surreal – yet too real – moment on Saturday Night Live tonight, Jessica Chastain deliberately broke character as a talk show host to lament, what even matters anymore? Chastain ostensibly plays host Veronica Elders, quizzing contestants played by Cecily Strong, Kate McKinnon, and Kenan Thompson on whether newsworthy things President Trump has said or done recently even rate among his base or the media today. Not surprisingly, nothing does. Even hypothetical situations, however objectively horrible, don't matter. "The president fires Robert Mueller, the very man investigating him for treason.
