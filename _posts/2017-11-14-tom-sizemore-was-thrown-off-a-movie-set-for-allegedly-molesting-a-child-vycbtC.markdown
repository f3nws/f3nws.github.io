The now-55-year-old actor, infamous for domestic battery convictions, on-set outbursts and a nearly lifelong battle with drugs, remained one of Hollywood's busiest character actors until Monday, when THR , citing multiple named sources who were present, published one of the most disturbing accounts of sexual misconduct since Harvey Weinstein.
