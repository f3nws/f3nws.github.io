Everyone loves karaoke, but isn’t it time those lame old karaoke machines got an update? Meet the ROXI Electric Jukebox , a very cool little gadget that’s actually five devices in one. It lets you sing karaoke style duets along with the original artists, it streams millions of songs with no ads, it lets you tun into thousands of different radio stations from around the world, it has an awesome built-in name that tune game, and it also serves as a sound machine to help you meditate or fall asleep faster. All that in one tiny box with a very cool motion remote. Here are some highlights from the product page: ROXI offers 5 great music entertainment experiences, direct to your TV and home speakers: 1. UNLIMITED MUSIC - instant access to millions of tracks, including all the latest hits, artists and curated playlists – all ad free 2. WORLDWIDE RADIO – Tune into 1000s of stations and programs from across the globe 3. SING WITH THE STARS - Karaoke-style singing, duet with the original artist and never miss a beat with on-screen lyrics 4. NAME THAT TUNE – The ultimate music trivia game 5. SOUND MACHINE – audio therapy, perfect for yoga, meditation and getting a baby to sleep Comes with Smart Voice Search to help you find what you’re looking for instantly Includes ROXI wireless motion controller, with built-in mic for Voice Search and singing 1 year premium music pass included – gives you instant access to all the content ad free. 2nd year costs only $52 every year after
