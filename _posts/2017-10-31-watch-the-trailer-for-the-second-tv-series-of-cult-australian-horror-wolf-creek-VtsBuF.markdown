The cult Australian horror film which we're pretty sure would put any tourist off from visiting the country, Wolf Creek , returned as a television series late last year. Well, it's back again for a six-part second season, set to premiere on December 15 on Australian streaming service Stan. The announcement was made with a trailer, just in time for Halloween. The premise is somewhat the same as previous iterations of the series, but this time it's an entire busload of "soft tourists," who find themselves running into trouble with the frightening Mick Taylor (played by John Jarratt) in the middle of the country's outback.
