First, Silicon Valley disrupted the juice industry with a $400 machine that basically just squeezes a bag of juice. Then it bravely innovated with a $1,000 device that infuses water with tea, much like a metal tea infuser, but at 100 times the price. Alas, both are officially dead. Juicero went belly up in September , and now Teforia has gone to that big recycling bin in the sky.
