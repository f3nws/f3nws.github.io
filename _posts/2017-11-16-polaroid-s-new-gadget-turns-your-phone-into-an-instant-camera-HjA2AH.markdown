Instagram addicts may now have a new reason to give Motorola's Moto Z another look. Polaroid and Motorola have teamed up on a new Moto Mod for the company's Z series of phones. The Polaroid Insta-Share Printer is an instant camera accessory that turns the Moto Z into a tiny photo printer. SEE ALSO: Dongles, ranked It clips onto the back of the phone and connects to the Moto Z's camera (the Mod also has a dedicated shutter button to make snapping photos even easier) to turn the phone into a tiny Polaroid camera. You can also use the accessory to print out photos you've already taken, like your favorite Instagram shots and other pics you have stored on your phone. Speaking of Instagram, the Polaroid Mod has an accompanying app that will let you add text and filters to your pics before you print them. Read more...
