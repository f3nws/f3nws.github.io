What are your friends liking on Instagram? Whatever it is, it's about to land in your feed. Instagram is quietly rolling out a new feature that will recommend posts for you based on the activity of the accounts you follow. "Recommended for You" posts will sit in their own section, accessible through a thumbnail above your main feed, according to TechCrunch . SEE ALSO: Instagram has a cool new feature that only appears after you wait 5 seconds So, how does Instagram determine which posts will appear in the Recommended for You section? According to Instagram's help section , the posts are suggested according to content liked by the accounts you follow. If you see something you don't like, you can temporarily hide "Recommended for You" posts by tapping the camera icon above the post and selecting "Hide." Read more...
