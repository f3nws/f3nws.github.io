Well Get Out fans, we've got some bad news — and some good news. First, let's start with the bad news: In a preview for his CBS Sunday Morning interview with Tracy Smith , the comedian and Get Out director announced he was done with acting. "That’s the idea... "Acting is just nowhere near as fun for me as directing."
