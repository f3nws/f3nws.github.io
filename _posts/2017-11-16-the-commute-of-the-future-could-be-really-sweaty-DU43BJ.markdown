The "Idea Train" is pulling into the station and with it comes workout stations, a children's lounge, and relaxation compartments. Deutsche Bahn, a German rail system, recently revealed its concept train of the future . It includes many amenities because the company anticipates future trains will be competing with self-driving cars . The appeal of the train as a place to read and work will be eliminated when riders can do the same thing in an autonomous car.
