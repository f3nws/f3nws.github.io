Tom Clancy's Ghost Recon Wildlands launched as a somewhat uninspired game but subsequent updates have greatly enhanced the experience. The competitive multiplayer mode is about to get even better. Recently, the title got an expansion called Ghost War. Ghost War is a player vs. player mode which features tense team-based matches. Many gamers thought that the expansion wouldn't be updated with new content as it's a minor part of the game. However, Ubisoft surprised everyone with an expansive plan today.
