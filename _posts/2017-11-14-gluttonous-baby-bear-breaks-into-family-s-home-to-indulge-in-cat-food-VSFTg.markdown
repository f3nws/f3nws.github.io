A family of bears recently broke into a home in Monrovia, California, raiding the place for food. The family admits that they often leave the sliding glass door cracked in order to let their cats in and out. Apparently, that small opening was just big enough for a large bear cub to slip inside. The homeowner managed to captured the bear break-in on video, urging one of the cubs that was in the home eating their cats' food to leave.
