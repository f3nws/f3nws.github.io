For a show involving cosmic fungus and a giant tardigrade, Star Trek: Discovery has been pretty serious this season. That's not a bad thing. But Sunday night's episode — called "Magic to Make the Sanest Man go Mad" — marked a glorious return to the campy, cheesy version of Star Trek that made other series so much fun.
