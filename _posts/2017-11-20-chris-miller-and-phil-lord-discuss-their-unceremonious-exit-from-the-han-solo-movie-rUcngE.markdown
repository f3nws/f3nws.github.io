Getting fired is no fun, but for former Han Solo movie directors Chris Miller and Phil Lord, the glass is half full. The pair were unceremoniously removed from Solo: A Star Wars Story in June , three weeks before filming was due to be finished. A statement at the time cited "creative differences" as the reason for their departure.
