There are few among us who will truly experience the world in all its kaleidoscopic possibility as Springfield hero Ralph Wiggum. But there are some who will attempt to live vicariously through the sensory delights of The Simpsons favourite. In this case, they are YouTuber Andrew Rea, whose channel Binging with Babish specialises in recreating food from pop culture and Rhett McLaughlin and Link Neal of comedy talk show Good Mythical Morning . The three cooks teamed up to tackle Wiggum's sandwich masterpiece, containing crayons (not thumbtacks, thankfully). It's the sadly rejected entry from the Auntie Ovenfresh Bakeoff in episode 2 of Season 16, "All's Fair in Oven War." Read more...
