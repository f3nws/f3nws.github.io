Actor Robert Guillaume, the butler of popular TV series Soap and the voice of baboon Rafiki in The Lion King , has died aged 89 at his home in Los Angeles. SEE ALSO: Prince George's favourite film proves once and for all he just can't wait to be king His wife Donna told The Associated Press the actor died of complications of prostrate cancer, which he battled for years. Guillaume starred as the butler Benson DuBois in Soap from 1977 to 1981 — for which he won an Emmy for outstanding supporting actor — and later in the spinoff sitcom Benson , from 1979 to 1986.
