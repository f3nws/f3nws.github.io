It's not William Shatner's spoken word cover of "Rocket Man," but it's pretty catchy. Tesla founder Elon Musk has found himself the muse for a crestfallen new tribute song, dubbed "Elon Musk Is Making Me Sad." Posted on Soundcloud and first spotted by Wired , the track comes from Matt Sharp, former bassist for Weezer and current frontman for The Rentals. A lugubrious tale of woe, unbridled jealousy and eventual awe, the song follows rival neighbours Sharp and "Musk," from two households, both alike in middle class dignity.
