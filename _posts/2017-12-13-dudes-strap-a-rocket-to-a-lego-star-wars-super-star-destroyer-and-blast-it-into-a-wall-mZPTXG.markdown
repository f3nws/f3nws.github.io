The only thing better than building a gigantic Lego structure is watching it get unnecessarily blasted to bits. After building a Lego Star Wars Super Star Destroyer consisting of 3,152 pieces, YouTuber David Windestal decided to do a quick breakdown, with a little help from a powerful rocket. Using a custom mount, Windestal was able to strap the 8-pound spaceship to a sled and rocket, which accelerated the craft to a speed of 62 mph in just a few short seconds.
