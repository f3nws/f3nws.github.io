Skiing star Lindsey Vonn suffered a back injury during a World Cup super-G race in Switzerland Saturday, two days after she criticized President Donald Trump in an interview about next year's Winter Olympics.. Vonn crossed the finish line in obvious distress, almost one second behind the then-leader, and slumped to the snow.
