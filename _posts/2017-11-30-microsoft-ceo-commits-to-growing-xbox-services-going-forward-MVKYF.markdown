During Microsoft's Annual Meeting of Shareholders, CEO Satya Nadella outlined its interest in expanding its gaming services. Microsoft's gaming efforts have continued to grow in recent years, making for a major turnaround of the Xbox One brand. Over four years since the console's launch, the Xbox brand has continued to climb to its former glory. And coming off the debut of the Xbox One X, the company's most powerful console to date, the reception to its latest endeavors is looking strong. Chief Executive Officer Satya Nadella has now spoken on the successes and potential of the Xbox brand, as a part of Microsoft's Annual Meeting of Shareholders on Wednesday. As reported by DualShockers, Nadella reaffirmed the firm's commitment to gaming going forward, outlining plans for continued expansion into the market.
