Can you believe that it’s been nearly 30 years since Capcom first brought one-on-one brawling to video game arcades with its iconic title, Street Fighter? But the publisher has another treat in store for fans in 2018: a collection of 12 popular old SF games originally released between 1987 and 1999, starting with the very first Street Fighter – ported to modern consoles and PC to celebrate three decades of delighting fighting game buffs.
