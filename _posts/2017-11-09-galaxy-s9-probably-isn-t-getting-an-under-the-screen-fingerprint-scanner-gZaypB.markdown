There’s a chance Samsung users won’t be getting a fingerprint reader on the next Galaxy device, though you may get one sometime in the next few years. As S8 users know, the fingerprint sensor on the back of the phone can be a source of irritation. It was one of the cons in my colleague Napier Lopez’s look at the Note 8. If you thought maybe Samsung would learn its lesson for the S9, then you might be disappointed.
