Tesla's giant battery is already proving its worth, bailing out a coal power station nearly 620 miles (1,000 kilometres) away. The world's largest lithium ion battery, installed to alleviate South Australia's severe energy issues, was put to the test when it supplied emergency power to one of Australia's biggest coal units, reports Renew Economy . According to RE , brown coal-powered Loy Yang Power Station experienced an unexpected loss of 560MW (that is a lot ) on Dec. 14.
