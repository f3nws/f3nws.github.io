Recruiting spies is seldom a public exercise, but Australia's intelligence agency is doing something different. The Australian Secret Intelligence Service (ASIS), equivalent to the CIA and Britain's MI6, has launched an online interactive test to discover people who are cut out for the job. SEE ALSO: How to check if your Airbnb host is secretly filming you Through a series of visual and aural exercises, the test seeks to identify suitable applicants that have the perception and empathy to work in the service.
