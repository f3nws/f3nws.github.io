After taking home his first Golden Globe, and being the first male Asian American to win for Best Actor in a TV Series, Master of None star Aziz Ansari celebrated in his own way. Not with more Italian food, although he thanked Italy for its delicious cuisine in his acceptance speech. Instead, Ansari and his co-star and "big bud" Eric Wareheim paid a little visit to In-N-Out for a celebratory meal.
