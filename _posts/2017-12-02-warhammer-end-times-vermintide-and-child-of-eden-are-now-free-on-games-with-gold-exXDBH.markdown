Warhammer: End Times – Vermintide and Child of Eden are now free on Games with Gold. Games with Gold is a program tied to your Xbox Live Gold membership which gives you access to some games at no cost with your subscription. While you keep all Xbox 360 games even if your subscription elapses, you have to maintain Gold status if you want continued access to the Xbox One titles. Warhammer: Vermintide is a first-person action game which tasks you with ridding a city of giant rat-like creatures. The once proud city of Ubersreik now stands in ruin at the hands of the dreaded Skaven army. Few have survived their ruthless invasion. You team up with three friends and work together to thin out the ratmen's forces and drive them out of the city. ...
