It'll rent VHS tapes, along with Blu-ray discs and DVDs. Image: alamo drafthouse The company knows watching VHS tapes isn't much of an option without a VCR, so the antiquated machines will be available for rent along with a free rental for HDMI-to-RCA adapters.
