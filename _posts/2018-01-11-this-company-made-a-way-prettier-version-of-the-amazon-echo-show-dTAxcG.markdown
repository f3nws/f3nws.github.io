The market is full of awesome smart devices. But when it comes to looks, many of them are hardly inspiring. But it doesn't have to be that way, as demonstrated by Brilliant Control, a 2018 CES Innovation Award winner. Brilliant is a sleek, futuristic wall panel that doubles as a smart light switch and a smart home hub. Brilliant, the product's parent company, announced at CES that the product will be integrated with Amazon Alexa.
