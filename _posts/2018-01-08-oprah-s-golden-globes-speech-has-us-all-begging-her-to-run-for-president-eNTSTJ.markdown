We knew Sunday's Golden Globes would be an emotional affair, but we didn't expect it to kick off Oprah's political career. Now, to be fair, when Oprah Winfrey gave her extremely moving speech upon accepting the Cecil B. DeMille award, she didn't explicitly say that she would run for president in 2020. But as she imparted hope, wisdom, and the promise of a brighter future, that didn't stop many people from wishing she would. Actually, scratch that. Many people just decided to believe that she's running and leaving it at that. And who can blame them.
