The British capital saw 454 acid attacks reported last year, up from 261 in 2015 and 166 the year before. Home Secretary Amber Rudd announced new proposals in October to force people carrying acid to prove they have a legitimate reason, taking the same approach used to tackle knife violence where possession of the weapon alone is criminalised.
