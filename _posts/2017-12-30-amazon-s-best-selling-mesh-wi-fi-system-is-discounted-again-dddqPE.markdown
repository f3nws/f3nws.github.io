The Google Wifi mesh wireless system has been Amazon’s best-selling mesh solution ever since it was released. It’s easy to understand why, of course — it’s simple, it works well, it’s Google, and it’s priced below most rival mesh systems at just $299 for three Google Wifi routers that cover up to 4,500 square feet. Well if you think $299 is a good price, you’ll be happy to learn that the system is currently discounted on Amazon, so now is the perfect time to upgrade if you’re still using an old single-router solution. Here’s what you should know from the product page: A new type of connected system for seamless Wi-Fi coverage throughout your home, helping eliminate dead zones and buffering Replaces your current router, and works your modem and internet service. It’s compatible with major internet service providers including Comcast, Time Warner, and Verizon Fios A single Wifi point covers up to 1,500 sq. ft, a set of three covers homes up to 4,500 sq. ft.
