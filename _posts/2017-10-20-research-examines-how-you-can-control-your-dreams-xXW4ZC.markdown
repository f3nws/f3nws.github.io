Lucid dreams are fun, but inducing has largely been a matter of trial and error. You might have an easier experience next time you hit the hay, off the back of new research by Australia's University of Adelaide that looked into more effective lucid dream induction techniques. Behind the study was Denholm Aspy from the university's School of Psychology.
