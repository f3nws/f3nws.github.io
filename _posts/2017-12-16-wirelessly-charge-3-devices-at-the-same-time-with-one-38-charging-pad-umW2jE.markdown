Apple’s upcoming AirPower wireless charging pad looks pretty terrific because it can charge three devices at once. Significantly less terrific, however, is the rumored $200 price tag. If you’re a big-time Apple fan and have pretty much everything the company makes, we’re sure you’ll happily add AirPower to your arsenal. But if you’d rather save $160, the Kaci QI Wireless Charger with 3 Wireless Charging Ports can also charge three devices at a time. Actually, it can charge five at a time — three wirelessly and two via USB ports on the side of the device. Here are some key details from the product page: Multiple charging ports:you can charge 3 Qi-enabled phones at the same time with 3 wireless charging ports,and this charger also has 2 USB ports,so you can charge 5 phones in total. Note: Remove protective cases to ensure effective charging.(DOES NOT charge Apple Watch) Premium Design: compact build ensure simple and convenient charging. This Wireless Charger works with fully QI-ENABLE devices like iPhone 8, 8 Plus, iPhone X, Galaxy Note 8, Galaxy S8, Galaxy S8 Plus, Galaxy S7/S7 Edge, Galaxy S6/S6 Edge, Nexus 4, Nexus 5, Lumia920 and other Qi-enabled devices (Don't recommend use it on all battery case) It also works with Samsung Note 3/ Note 2/Galaxy S3/S4 with special Receiver.(The package DOES NOT include charging receiver) Package includes: Wireless Charger/ AC Adapter/User Manual / Warranty Card (standard: 1 year)
