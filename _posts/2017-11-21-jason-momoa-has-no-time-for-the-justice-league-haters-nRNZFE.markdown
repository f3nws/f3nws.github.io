Momoa clearly approached the role with all the joie de vivre he brings to his day-to-day existence — which, judging by his social media, is often spent throwing axes , drinking Guinness and hanging out with his beautiful friends — so he's pretty "bummed" that the internet (and box office receipts) started raining on his parade by pointing out that the movie is .. not great .
