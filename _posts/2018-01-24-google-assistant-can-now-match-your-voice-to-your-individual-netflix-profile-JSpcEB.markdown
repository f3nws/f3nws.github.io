Netflix has long been available on Google Assistant, but now Google has added the ability to match individual voices with their own Netflix profiles. Until now, only one main paired Netflix account was accessible on Google Home devices, able to throw binge-worthy series to your TV using a Chromecast. But if you're in a busy household with conflicting television tastes and watching rates, it can be a little annoying using just one account.
