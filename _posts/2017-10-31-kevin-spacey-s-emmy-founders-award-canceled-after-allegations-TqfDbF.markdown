The International Academy of Television Arts & Sciences has decided to withdraw a forthcoming accolade destined for Kevin Spacey. The organization, which awards Emmys to television programs outside the U.S., said in a statement Tuesday it would no longer honor Spacey at its 2017 event, following accusations of sexual misconduct against him.
