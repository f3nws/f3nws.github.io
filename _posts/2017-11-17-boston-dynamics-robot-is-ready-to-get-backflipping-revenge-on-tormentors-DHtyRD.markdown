Atlas has been working on its moves. Earlier this week, Boston Dynamics shared a video of its dog-like SpotMini smoothly trotting through a backyard, kneeling, and looking right at the camera. It was creepy. The only clue about what's going on with Boston Dynamics in the latest video is the video headline: "What's new, Atlas?" It seems to hint that the robot is undergoing upgrades and major improvements.
