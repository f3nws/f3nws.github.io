Many months ago Square Enix promised Final Fantasy 15 players they could soon enjoy the rich world in a multiplayer setting. After a few delays, that day is finally here. Beginning today, Final Fantasy 15 players can band together and join the Kingsglaive elite force in the game's first-ever online multiplayer expansion, called Comrades. Comrades takes place after the events of Chapter 13 in the main game. According to Square Enix, the expansion puts fans in the shoes of the remaining Kingsglaive survivors as they fight to restore Light to the world during Noctis' absence. Other members of Noctis' crew including Gladiolus, Prompto and Ignis will be playable in future updates. ...
