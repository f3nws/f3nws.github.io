Unless you live in Japan, fried chicken and Christmas aren't really things that go together. Regardless, KFC in New Zealand is trying to make it a thing with a series of tree decorations for the unrelenting chicken fan. SEE ALSO: People are decorating their pineapples as Christmas trees The set of five decorations features a drumstick, chips (a.k.a. fries), a wing, a bucket of fried chicken, and of course, Colonel Sanders. Packed "beautifully" in a black presentation box, the fast food chain will release these in New Zealand stores in early-December. Sadly, it appears you can only win them on the chain's Facebook page.
