Your credit card is about to get a lot smarter. SEE ALSO: The best tech of CES 2018 Dynamics Inc. and Sprint announced at CES 2018 a reprogrammable payment card that can be used for a bunch of different accounts: debit, credit, loyalty, and any other transaction that uses a magnetic strip. But this card has an extra innovation: It's one of the first cards with a cellphone chip inside, and it's coming to U.S. banks. The idea of a reprogrammable credit card isn't all that spectacular or new. The company Plastc famously disintegrated after raising $9 million through a popular crowdfunding campaign. But this card could be different because its creator, Dynamics Inc., is already working with banks around the world using similar technology. Read more...
