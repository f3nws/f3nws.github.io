The NAACP has issued a national travel advisory warning people about flying with American Airlines. In a press release , the social justice organization said it was "alerting travelers — especially African-Americans — to exercise caution, in that booking and boarding flights on American Airlines could subject them to disrespectful, discriminatory or unsafe conditions." The advisory was dated Oct. 24 and stands "until further notice."
